Frequently Asked Questions
What does the Accident & Sickness Hospitalization plan cover?

The Accident & Sickness Hospitalization plan covers daily benefits for hospital stays (up to $400/day), ICU stays (up to $1,600/day), outpatient surgery (up to $4,000), and ambulance services (up to $800).

How long does the recovery benefit last in the Accident & Sickness Hospitalization plan?

The recovery benefit lasts for the number of days you were hospitalized (up to 3 times the number of days hospitalized, for a maximum of $400/day) if fully disabled after hospitalization.

What is included in the Outpatient Essentials Accident & Sickness coverage?

The Outpatient Essentials plan covers up to $1,000 per outpatient surgery (up to 4 surgeries per year), rehabilitative therapy, and medical appliance reimbursement.

What types of accidents are covered under the Accidental Death Essentials plan?

The Accidental Death Essentials plan covers accidental death by common carrier (up to $400,000) and all other causes of accidental death (up to $200,000), along with dismemberment benefits.

What does the Accident & Sickness Disability policy cover?

The Accident & Sickness Disability policy provides up to $3,000/month for total disability. Partial disability benefits offer up to $3,000/month and Return-to-work assistance provides up to $1,500/month for up to 30 days.

What is the lump-sum payout for the Critical Illness plan?

The Critical Illness plan offers lump-sum payments of $10,000, $25,000, $50,000, $75,000, or $100,000 for major illnesses like cancer, heart attack, stroke, and more.

What are the two Cancer Care Protector plans?

The Cancer Care Protector offers two plans: Gold Plan ($100/day) and Platinum Plan ($200/day), both providing daily benefits to cover cancer treatment-related hospital stays.

How many outpatient surgeries are covered under the Outpatient Essentials plan?

The Outpatient Essentials plan covers up to 4 outpatient surgeries per year, with reimbursement up to $1,000 per surgery.

Can I receive disability benefits if I’m only partially disabled?

Yes, the Accident & Sickness Disability policy provides up to $3,000/month for partial disability, up to 30 days.

What types of critical illnesses are covered under the Critical Illness plan?

The Critical Illness plan covers cancer, stroke, heart attack, and other serious health issues, with varying benefit levels depending on the plan selected.
